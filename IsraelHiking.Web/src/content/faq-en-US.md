# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Mapeak?
Mapeak is an outdoor navigation app built for real adventures — hiking, cycling, trail running, and offroad driving. It works online and fully offline using community-verified maps that update daily.

## Which platforms does Mapeak support?
Mapeak is available on iOS and Android. You can also plan routes on the web and sync them with your mobile device.

## Is Mapeak free to use?
Yes! Mapeak Free gives you unlimited route planning, elevation analysis, community routes, and points of interest search at no cost. Mapeak Pro adds offline map downloads and offline navigation for $29.99/year.

## How do I download maps for offline use?
Open the app, go to Manage Offline Maps, and tap the region you want to download. Once downloaded, maps work with no internet connection for days of navigation.

## Do I need Mapeak Pro for offline maps?
Yes. Unlimited offline map downloads and full offline navigation require a Mapeak Pro subscription at $29.99/year. You can subscribe directly inside the iOS or Android app.

## How current are the offline maps?
Community updates flow in daily. When you re-download a region, you get the latest trail changes, closures, and new paths contributed by the global Mapeak community.

## Can I use offline maps to save battery?
Yes — vector maps stored on your device are rendered locally, which significantly reduces GPS and network radio usage. Mapeak Pro includes a battery optimization mode for long trails.

## What activity types does Mapeak support?
Mapeak supports hiking, cycling (including MTB), trail running, and offroad driving. Each activity type uses routing rules and community data tailored to that sport.

## Can I plan a route on my computer and use it on my phone?
Yes. Plan your route on the web, save it to the cloud, and open it in the app. You can also export routes as GPX files for use in other apps.

## How do I reverse or edit a route?
Open the route in the route editor and use the Reverse Route option in the route menu. You can also drag route points, add new segments, or split and merge routes.

## Can I record my own tracks and upload them?
Yes. Start a recording from the app and when you finish, you can upload it to OSM or save it locally. Enable automatic upload in the advanced settings.

## Where does Mapeak's map data come from?
Mapeak is built on OpenStreetMap (OSM) data — a global, community-maintained map that anyone can edit. Millions of contributors add and verify trails, roads, and points of interest.

## How do I report a map error?
Use the Report an Issue button in the app, or edit the map directly on OpenStreetMap. Corrections are reflected in Mapeak's daily map refreshes.

## Can I add or edit trails and points of interest?
Yes! Log in with your OSM account and use the Edit This Map Using OSM button. You can also upload recorded tracks to help fill in unmapped routes.

## How do I subscribe to Mapeak Pro?
Open the Mapeak app on iOS or Android and follow the upgrade prompt. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store or Google Play at $29.99/year.

## How do I manage or cancel my subscription?
Go to Manage Subscriptions inside the app, or manage it directly through your iOS App Store or Google Play account settings.

## Can I delete my account?
Yes. Go to Settings and choose Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated cloud data.

## Is my data private?
Please review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service for full details on data handling. Routes marked as private are only visible to you.
